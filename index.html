About

I spend my days helping brands communicate better — and my spare time noticing which products respect people's time and which don't.

I gravitate toward minimalist, modern, easy-to-use things. Not because minimal is trendy, but because good design removes friction. When something just works, you stop thinking about it. That's the point.

How I Think

Perfectionist, but not perfect. I'm not chasing flawless — I'm chasing great. Do what's great, serve what people actually need, and keep it lovely. That's the whole philosophy.
